Our Mission

We live in an age of unprecedented speed and convenience; where apps exist for nearly every social interaction. Dating, cinema, education, video games; all can be accessed effortlessly from any device. People expect this degree of ease and speed in every facet of their lives. But there is a very popular arena of social interaction where such convenience is strikingly absent: real life, pick-up basketball. Courts are fickle, and can often be deserted, overcrowded or occupied by players far above your skill level. Online gaming has led players to expect convenient, immediate matchmaking systems. This got us thinking: with dating apps more popular than ever, and games like Fortnite matching up dozens of players within minutes, why isn’t there an equivalent system for pick-up basketball? That’s what WeBall brings to the game.
